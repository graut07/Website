WEBINAR ON “IMPORTANCE OF CYBER SECURITY” - 13th August 2022

SNDT ACM Students' Chapter organized a webinar on the "Importance of Cyber Security", by Mr. Pravin Swami on 13th August, 2022 on Google Meet for the duration of 5:00p.m to 7:00 p.m. Mr. Pravin Swami has worked as an Cyber Cell expert for Maharashtra Police for 9 years. He has worked on many cases like Cryptocurrency frauds, bitcoin fraud which happened in 2000, Social media frauds for money, marriage frauds,International call center frauds, etc.

There was an overwhelming participation of 70 students along with our new Chapter Sponsor Dr. Anita Morey for the webinar. The Chief Guest explained in detail about the types of Cyber Crimes and hazards of online transactions. ×

" WELCOME CEREMONY "

“WELCOME REPRESENTATIVES” EVENT - 25th August 2022

SNDT ACM Students' Chapter organized a "Welcome Representatives" event on 25th August, 2022 in the Conference Room for the duration of 1:00p.m to 5:00 p.m. The event started with the introduction of the representatives followed by the speech of our Chapter Coordinator, Dr. Anita Morey ma'am. She congratulated the representatives for joining our council and talked about the rules and presentation for a Chapter member. She also encouraged the listeners to participate actively and to work as a team.

There was an overwhelming participation of 31 representatives along with our respected Athya ma'am who graced the event. After the orientation and speeches of Current and Elect Chairperson, Elect Vice Chairperson and Elect General Secretary, the event took a fun turn. The representatives were made to play fun technical games such as Technical Hunt where they had to crack technical clues to reach the destination prize. The event at last witnessed happy faces from every participant perfectly suiting the values of a perfect and united council!

"Teacher's Day"

SNDT ACM Students' Chapter celebrated the occasion of "Teachers' Day" on 5 th September,2022 in our College Campus for the duration of 1:00p.m to 2:45 p.m. The celebration involved the presence of our elects, heads and representatives

There was an active participation of our representatives who made greeting cards for each and every teaching and non-teaching staff of our college along with a message as to why they are grateful to them. After collecting all cards, the council members started in teams to distribute the cards to our teachers and also to our respected Principal ma’am.

All the faculty and non-teaching staff appreciated and loved our efforts and wished us good luck for our future events. So by taking the blessings of our gurus, SNDT ACM Students’ Chapter signed off from this celebration.
